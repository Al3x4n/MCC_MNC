Serbia
Communications of 11.IV. 2018:
On the occasion of the 70th anniversary of the foundation of the Amateur Radio Union of Vojvodina, the Serbian
Administration authorizes radio stations of the Amateur Radio Union of Vojvodina to use the special call signs
YT70SRV and YU70SRV from 1 May to 31 December 2018.
On the occasion of the 100th anniversary of the unification of Vojvodina with the Kingdom of Serbia, the Serbian
Administration authorizes radio stations of the Amateur Radio Union of Vojvodina to use the special call sign
YU100APV from 1 May to 31 December 2018.
On the occasion of the 40th anniversary of the foundation of Radio Section “Kozara”, as member of Amateur
Radio Club “Novi Beograd”, the Serbian Administration authorizes the Radio section “Kozara” to use the special
call sign YU40AAV from 1 June to 15 October 2018.
